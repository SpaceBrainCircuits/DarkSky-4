This firmware version does the following:

1. When Decay knob is fully CCW, this will disable the decay stage of the ADSR envelope, eliminating the popping sound.
2. Added CC messaging for the following controls.
	Portamento - CC5
	Detune - CC16
	Attack - CC17
	Decay - CC18
	Sustain - CC19
	Release - CC20
	Breath Control - CC2
